20 Years of Industrial Expertise

Founded in 2004, Ridgesun began as a small engineering firm specializing in HVAC systems for manufacturing facilities. Recognizing the need for specialized air filtration in industrial settings, we developed the PureRing series—systems specifically engineered for the unique challenges of factory environments.

Today, our products are trusted by manufacturers, laboratories, and industrial facilities worldwide, with a focus on North American markets where air quality regulations are most stringent.
