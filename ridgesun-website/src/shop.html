Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I replace filters?

HEPA H13: Every 6 months. MERV 11: Every 3 months. In high-dust environments, replace more frequently.

Will Amazon ship internationally?

Yes, these filters are available for international shipping through Amazon Global.

Are these filters washable?

MERV 11 filters have a washable option. HEPA H13 filters are not washable and must be replaced.

What if I use non-genuine filters?

Using non-genuine filters may void your warranty and reduce system performance.
