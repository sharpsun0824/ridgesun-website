Quick Start Guide

Like IKEA instructions, but for industrial air scrubbers. Simple, visual, effective.

1

Unpack & Inspect

Remove all packaging materials. Check for any shipping damage. Verify all components are present: unit, power cord, user manual.

2

Position Unit

Place on level surface near power outlet. Ensure at least 2 feet clearance on all sides for optimal airflow.

3

Connect Power

Plug into grounded outlet (110-240V AC). Press power button. Green LED indicates ready status.

4

Test Operation

Select fan speed (Low/Med/High). Verify airflow from exhaust. Check for unusual noises.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about PureRing products.

How do I know when to replace the HEPA filter?

Replace HEPA filters every 12-18 months under normal use, or when: • Airflow decreases noticeably • Filter indicator light turns red (1000i/1000ie models) • After heavy use in dusty environments We recommend keeping spare filters on hand for continuous operation.

Can I use PureRing outdoors?

PureRing is designed for indoor use. For outdoor applications: • Use under covered areas only (no direct rain) • Ensure proper drainage if used in damp conditions • Consider our weather-resistant models for permanent outdoor installation • Always use GFCI-protected outlets outdoors

What's the difference between 1000, 1000i, and 1000ie?

All three models share the same H13 HEPA filtration (99.97% @ 0.3µm). Differences: • 1000: Manual controls, basic operation • 1000i: Digital touch controls, LCD display • 1000ie: WiFi/Bluetooth, mobile app, real-time air quality monitoring Choose based on your need for connectivity and monitoring features.

How do I clean the exterior of my PureRing?

For exterior cleaning: 1. Unplug unit from power 2. Use soft, damp cloth with mild detergent 3. Avoid abrasive cleaners or solvents 4. Do not spray water directly on unit 5. Dry thoroughly before use For stubborn stains, use isopropyl alcohol on a cloth (test small area first).
